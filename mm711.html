Cooler Master MM711

The CoolerMaster Mastermouse MM711 is the RGB edition of CoolerMaster's foray into the lightweight market.

It boasts a shocking 60 grams, and puts a twist on the lightweight market, bringing a wider shape to the mainstream market.

It also has various other competitive features such as PTFE skates, an ultraweave cable, and of course, its lightweight shell.

Due to its small size, it's best suited for fingertip grip or claw grip and any hand size should be able to use it with these grip types.

This mouse does not compromise on much.

Dimensions

  • Lenght: 11.66 cm
  • Width: 5.45 cm
  • Height: 3.83 cm
  • Weight: 60 Grams
  • Shape: False Ambidextrous

Cooler Master MM711

Features

  • Connectivity:Wired - Fixed Ultraweave cable
  • Sensor: PixArt PMW3389
  • Buttons: OMRON, 20 million
  • Dpi: 200 – 16000, in steps of 100
  • Polling Rate in HZ: 125 / 500 / 1000
  • RGB Lighting: Yes, RGB on wheel and logo

Verdict

Pros

Cons

Conclusion
