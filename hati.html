Hati-S Ace Edition

G-Wolves has a very nice unique experience, they pack the mouse and accessories in a tin box which is something original to the company.

In the box you get: anti-slip grips, a detachable micro-USB cable, smelly packet and a brush.

I found it to feel very premium and it felt like I was unboxing a super expensive item.

It boasts a shocking 49 grams, and puts a twist on the lightweight market, bringing a wider shape to the mainstream market.

It also has various other competitive features such as PTFE skates, a detachable micro-usb ultraweave cable, and of course, its lightweight shell.

Due to its small size, it's best suited for a fingertip grip, and any hand size should be able to use it with this grip type.

Dimensions

  • Lenght: 11.3 cm
  • Width: 6.1 cm
  • Height: 4 cm
  • Weight: 49 Grams
  • Shape: False Ambidextrous

Verdict

Pros

  • Really great shape for fingertip
  • Amazing light weight that doesn’t sacrifice build quality
  • Mice feet are smooth and fast
  • Great coating/skins included

Cons

  • Side buttons are definitely less impressive than main clicks
  • Slight rattling when shaking the mouse
  • Driver software could use a bit more polish

Rating FPS Games

Conclusion

This mouse is very light at 48.5-52.5 grams but doesn’t sacrifice much in build quality apart from the minor bottom flex and some scroll wheel issues some reviewers have had, but on my copy there are no issues with scroll wheel.

I was hitting more flicks and my tracking was better, I play best with this mouse so it’s staying on my desk.

It has a great unboxing experience, different to most of the market. One thing I really like is the detachable cable, so it should be a lot easier to paracord as you don’t need to open up the mouse.

Overall, this is a really good mouse and is now my new main.
